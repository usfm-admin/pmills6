About Us

Put The Power Of Orange County’s Best CPA Firm To Work For You!

 

A licensed CPA since 1997, Patrice spent 7 years employed in the Tax Department as a Tax Manager for one of the Big 6 Firms – Arthur Andersen, LLP.

While at Arthur Andersen, Patrice offered tax and consulting services to a variety of individuals, businesses and entities. She was also a member of the estate planning division, assisting clients to reduce or eliminate their possible future estate taxes.

 

Patrice is a member of the AICPA (American Institute of Certified Public Accountants) and the CalCPA (California Society of Certified Public Accountants). She is also a Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor.

 

Patrice graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with an emphasis in Accounting from California State University, Long Beach.

 

Her Volunteer Work Includes: * Finance Committee of Corpus Christi Catholic Christian Community - Aliso Viejo * Volunteer as PTA Treasurer and Auditor   Patrice has the knowledge and experience to help you save the time and money, allowing you to grow your business and enjoy your life!   Patrice Mills CPA, P.C. employs a number of trusted associates to ensure your comfort and security regarding your tax and financial matter.
